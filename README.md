# Kwento

> *"Get your stories out of your head."*

**Kwento** is an AI-powered story development workspace that helps writers extract fragmented ideas from their minds and transform them into well-structured, polished story plans.

## What It Does

- **AI Conversation**: Talk through your ideas naturally; Kwento organizes them for you
- **Visual Story Canvas**: See your story as connected cards - characters, scenes, plot points
- **Character Bible**: Keep characters consistent with detailed profiles
- **Plot Hole Detection**: Catch story issues before you write
- **Structured Export**: Output story plans ready to guide your writing
